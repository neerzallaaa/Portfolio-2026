Undergraduate Thesis

Analysis and Service Quality Improvement Using Importance-Satisfaction Model and Gap Analysis

at PT Solo Grafika Utama

Research Abstract

This research aims to evaluate and improve the service quality at PT Solo Grafika Utama. By leveraging the Importance-Satisfaction Model (ISM) and Gap Analysis, the study identifies critical service attributes where customer expectations exceed the perceived performance. The findings provide actionable, data-driven recommendations for management to prioritize service improvements, optimize resource allocation, and ultimately enhance customer satisfaction.

Methodologies Used

Gap Analysis Importance-Satisfaction Model Statistical Data Processing Customer Satisfaction Index
